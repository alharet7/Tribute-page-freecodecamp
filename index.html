IRON MAN

Iron Man is a fictional character, but who said he couldn't be kind of real?

Why did I choose him?

  • He is a genius engineer and this is the main thing I chose him for.
  • He made his first suit out of simple equipment, which teaches you that if you want something, you can do it.
  • He appears to be proficient in mechanical engineering, electronics engineering, and software engineering, and because I love engineering, I have already studied these three engineering fields.
  • Because he always learns from his mistakes and always develops himself, and this is what a successful engineer does.
  • When he makes a mistake, he apologizes, and this is one of the qualities of good men, not just superheroes.
  • He runs one of the most efficient companies, and nowadays some engineers are doing it, like Elon Musk, Jeff Bezos, and others. And we can learn the good things from them.
  • Some of his superpowers are based on science that can be applied in our current days, and indeed some made such suits.
  • I know that I need a lot of time, knowledge, and hard work to achieve great things, but who knows where I can reach?
